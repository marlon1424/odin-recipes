Pancakes

Good old fashioned pancakes made from scratch.

This recipe can be made by anyone.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cup all purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 1 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Sift together flour, sugar, salt and baking powder
  2. Add melted butter and egg and mix altogether
  3. Lightly heat griddle or pan and pour pancake mixute onto. Heat until both sides are brown.
